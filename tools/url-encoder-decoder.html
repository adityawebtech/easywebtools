What is a URL Encoder and Decoder?

A URL Encoder and Decoder tool is an essential utility for web developers, digital marketers, bloggers, and anyone working with URLs or links. URLs (Uniform Resource Locators) often contain special characters that must be formatted correctly to be safely transmitted over the internet. These characters may include spaces, question marks, ampersands, hashtags, or even non-ASCII characters. To ensure URLs remain functional and error-free, we use encoding and decoding techniques.

URL encoding replaces unsafe ASCII characters with a "%" followed by two hexadecimal digits. For example, a space character becomes %20. This process is also known as percent-encoding. URL decoding, on the other hand, reverses this process and transforms encoded URLs back to their original readable form. It’s crucial when processing URLs in APIs, forms, redirects, and databases.
