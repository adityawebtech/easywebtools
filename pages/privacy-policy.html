Privacy Policy

At Toolswagon, accessible from https://toolswagon.site, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Toolswagon and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We want to be transparent about the data we collect. Generally, we do not collect any personally identifiable information unless voluntarily provided by the user (such as submitting a contact form or subscribing to a newsletter). The types of information we may collect include:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • IP address and general location
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URLs
  • Tool usage data (e.g., using our YouTube Thumbnail Downloader)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To improve, personalize, and expand our website
  • To understand and analyze how users interact with our tools
  • To improve user experience and functionality
  • To develop new tools, features, and services
  • To prevent fraud or misuse of our tools

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like most websites, Toolswagon uses cookies to store information such as visitor preferences and the pages they access. This information is used to optimize the user experience by customizing content based on browser type or other information.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. For detailed cookie control info, visit AboutCookies.org.

4. Google Analytics and Tracking

We use third-party services like Google Analytics to monitor site traffic and user behavior. These tools may collect IP addresses and behavior data anonymously for analytical purposes. For more on Google's privacy practices, see Google Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Links

Toolswagon tools and blog pages may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of other sites and encourage users to review them individually. External links are provided to add more value to your browsing experience.

6. Data Security

We implement standard security practices to protect your data. Although no online transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure, we take adequate steps to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Children's Privacy

Our tools and website are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Consent

By using our website and tools, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. We recommend checking this page occasionally to stay informed.
